# Privacy Policy

**App:** Heap
**Last updated:** May 13, 2026

---

## Overview

Heap is a mobile 2D platformer where players climb a community-built trash heap. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it's handled. We keep it simple because we collect very little.

---

## What We Collect

### Data you provide
- **Display name** — a name or nickname you choose for the leaderboard. You can use any name; we do not require your real name.

### Data collected automatically
- **Score and gameplay data** — your high score, height reached, and related in-game statistics, used to populate the leaderboard.
- **Device-generated identifier** — a random GUID generated on your device the first time you play. This is not tied to your identity and is used to associate your scores with your name across sessions.

### Google Play Games Services (if signed in)
If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, we receive your Google Play Games player ID and display name from Google. This is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Signing in is optional — the game is fully playable without it.

---

## What We Do NOT Collect

- Real name, email address, or contact information (unless you type one as your display name)
- Location data
- Device hardware identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
- Photos, microphone, or camera access
- Payment or financial information
- Browsing history or data from other apps

---

## How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Display name | Show on the in-game leaderboard |
| Score / gameplay stats | Populate the leaderboard; track your personal best |
| Random GUID | Link your scores to your display name across sessions |
| Google Play Games ID | Authenticate your session (if you sign in) |

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

---

## Data Storage

Your game data (name, scores, heap entries) is stored on our backend, which runs on Cloudflare's infrastructure. Cloudflare may process data in data centers globally. See [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) for details on their data handling.

Your local save data (placement history, settings) is stored on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

---

## Data Retention

We retain your leaderboard entry and scores for as long as the game is operational. If you want your data removed, contact us at the email below and we will delete your records.

---

## Children's Privacy

Heap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require any personal information to play. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information (e.g., a real name as a display name), contact us and we will remove it promptly.

---

## Your Rights

You may:
- **Access** your data — contact us to receive a copy of data associated with your GUID or display name.
- **Delete** your data — contact us and we will remove your leaderboard entries and save data from our servers.
- **Correct** your display name — you can update your display name in the game settings at any time.

---

## Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Our backend is protected by rate limiting and secret-gated admin routes. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable precautions appropriate to the sensitivity of the data (which is low).

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
