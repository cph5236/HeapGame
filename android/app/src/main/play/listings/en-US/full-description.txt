Heap is a community-built climbing platformer where the whole world builds one endless tower of trash — and you race to the top.

Every item you place stays behind. The heap you climb was shaped by the players before you, and everything you drop shapes the climb for everyone who comes next. No two runs are ever the same.

CLIMB THE HEAP
Pilot the character up a towering, ever-growing platformer stage. Time your jumps, scale ladders and beams, and dodge the enemies crawling the heap.

BUILD THE WORLD
Place items to grow the heap taller and forge new paths. Your contributions become permanent parts of the tower that every other player has to climb.

RACE FOR THE TOP
Chain kills, keep your pace, and climb higher to stack up your score. Every run is a fresh, arcade-style push for a new personal best.

UPGRADE EVERY RUN
Earn coins as you climb and spend them on run-based upgrades, consumables, and salvage boosts — speed, extra jumps, revives and more. Roguelite-style progression keeps every attempt feeling different.

CUSTOMIZE YOUR CLIMBER
Unlock dozens of cosmetics and make your trash item your own as you rise up the ranks.

COMPETE GLOBALLY
Climb the worldwide leaderboard and prove you belong at the top of the heap. Can you set the high score the whole community is chasing?

Free to play. Easy to pick up, tough to top. Jump in and start climbing.
